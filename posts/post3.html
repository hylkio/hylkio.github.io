Start of my journey learning SQL

How and why I did it?

Posted by Miro Sopanen on February 20, 2021

Past 2 weeks I have tried to learn SQL and databases in general. Here are a few tips and techniques for learning those concepts.

Watch youtube tutorials from the concept

This is in my opinion the best way to get a good overview of something you are learning. When I am learning almost any stuff related to tech, I try to find a good tutorial on it and watch it.

SQL is used for getting data from databases.

Reading arciles and documentations

The second way I have used to learn SQL and databases is reading articles and documentation about that stuff. Because databases are a quite "theoretical" concept of tech, I have found it helpful.
The best way to learn the concept well and in-depth is to create your projects and use what you've learned in your practices. I have personally made some problems and practice questions from the course I have been taking and I've found that helpful.

Technologies I try to learn and used for learning

When it comes to technologies that I have used for this data stuff, I list them down below:
- MySql
- SQL in general
- Windows 10 as the operating system
- Vim for editing SQL

Data and data bases are really important part of technology.

Although, everyone has their own ways to learn stuff. Others don't learn well by watching, but others don't learn by reading. I suppose you to try different ways of learning this concept of SQL and databases if you don't know well the way you learn.
